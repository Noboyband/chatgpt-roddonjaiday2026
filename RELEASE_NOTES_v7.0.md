# Roddonjai Day 2026 - Version 7.0.0

Release date: 2026-07-20

## Highlights

- Replaces the large floating text hearts with three supplied branded heart images.
- Randomizes the orange, dark blue, and bright blue heart images on every tap.
- Keeps the larger 48-100px floating-heart size for better mobile visibility.
- Adds an easy-to-tap background music control beside the speed indicator.
- Shows a blue sound icon and switches the button label between music on and off states.
